Song of the
Welkin Moon

Explore the concept and characters of Nod-Krai

The moon-blessed land located in the northwestern regions of Teyvat. The days are bitterly short, and the night stretches on like a dark, yawning maw. But even then, the light and song of the Welkin Moon will dance gently across the faces of visiting travelers. Up at ye towers, and let your lanterns blaze into the darkness! Until the day comes... when true moonlight bathes this land once more.
